The Dress Code

There is no formal dress code but we ask that you dress nicer than your everyday attire. Please keep in mind that the ceremony will be held outdoors (rain or shine), so we recommend dressing appropriately. The reception and dance will also take place outside, so feel free to plan for comfort while still looking your best!

Camping Details

  • On-site camping is available. You must let us know if you plan to camp. 
  • If you'd prefer to be camped near specific people we will do our best to accommodate. Add your request to the RSVP notes.
  • We ask that you arrive Friday or early Saturday morning at the latest to allow enough time to park and set up before the ceremony. If you arrive after 12:00 PM we will ask you to wait until the ceremony is over to finish setting up.
  • For those camping Friday night, we ask that you be respectful of the rehearsal activities and remain within your campsite.
  • There is no power available at the campsites, but you are welcome to bring a generator. Please be considerate of your neighbours when using it.
  • Fires are not permitted in the individual camping areas.
  • Motorized vehicles are not permitted beyond the parking area and campsites. Plan accordingly.
  • There will be signs and volunteers available to direct you once you arrive.
  • We ask that you clean up your camping area to help us keep the venue tidy. Please be mindful of the local wildlife and make sure food and waste are properly stored to avoid attracting uninvited guests.

Accommodations
